FSC Data Strategy & Data Architecture Design, Project Lead

FSC needed to modernize its data architecture to enhance data utilization in risk management, monitoring & evaluation, marketing, communication, and innovation, focusing on efficient data collection, processing, storage, retrieval, cataloging, classification, and enabling self-service analytics and visualization.

Led the formation of strategic alliances with the ISEAL Data Community of Practice (COP) to leverage the ISEAL Data Maturity Assessment as well as Microsoft to leverage the Intelligent Data Platform implementation framework.

Implementation in progress...

H12024

FSC Forest Atlas, Project Lead

FSC Members voted to make it mandatory for FSC International to collect geolocation data from all FSC Certified forests globally. This required FSC to develop a platform for collection and proactive management of forest data submitted by forest managers worldwide.

Throught rapid prototyping, leading a team of GIS developers and Data Analyst, we created and integrated the new FSC Forest Atlas with existing FSC IT systems for user authentication and data management.

A fully-functioning ArcGIS Hub application that is regarded as a minimum viable product (MVP), and an early adopter program (EAP) to get initial forest managers onboard.
