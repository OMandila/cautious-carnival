H22023

FSC Forest Atlas, Product Lead, 03/2023-08/2023

FSC Members voted to make it mandatory for FSC International to collect geolocation data from all FSC Certified forests globally. This required FSC to develop a platform for collection and proactive management of forest data submitted by forest managers worldwide.

Through rapid prototyping, leading a team of GIS developers and Data Analyst, we created and integrated the new FSC Forest Atlas with existing FSC IT systems for user authentication and data management.

  • A fully-functioning ArcGIS Hub application minimum viable product (MVP).
  • A data-driven early adopter program to get initial forest managers onboard.
